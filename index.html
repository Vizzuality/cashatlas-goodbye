Welcome to the Cash Atlas!

The Grand Bargain, our Global Framework for Action, and ODI's Counting Cash - Tracking humanitarian expenditure in cash based programming all identify an urgent need to improve how organisations track cash across the humanitarian sector. This recognises that cash and vouchers must be tracked within wider efforts to improve humanitarian data and reporting.

CaLP will be leading a Cash Tracking Working Group to support the development of common methods for tracking cash that can be built into wider systems.

In this changed context, it is appropriate to review the role and approach of the Cash Atlas, which currently operates as a standalone platform. We have made the difficult decision to take it offline while we do that. Existing data will be backed up and stored in a format that allows it to be accessed if needed.

Please contact Ruth McCormack (programmes@cashlearning.org) if you have any questions about this, or want to find out more about our plans.
